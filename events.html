Events

Every year, the team sets up displays and participates in many events on the university campus and in the community. Here is a list of events we have attended in the past. Come out to any of them in the future and you're sure to see us there!

Event Description
Baja SAE Competition - April

The Baja SAE (Society of Automotive Engineers) competition is a three-day event that puts the team's vehicle to the test. Examples of these tests include: acceleration, top speed, skid pull, hill climb and maneuverability events.

The main event in the competitions is the final grueling four-hour endurance race. Approximately 100 Baja cars will attempt to complete as many laps as possible, while overcoming obstacles

KW Santa Claus Parade - November

Decoration of vehicle and float which then participated in the KW Lion's Club Santa Claus Parade.
Fall Open House - November

Presentation of the vehicle, aimed at recruiting high school students to attend the University of Waterloo.
EngSoc Open House - October

Clubs and student teams: display of our team in the CPH foyer along with other Engineering teams and programs for students.
Homecoming Engineering Alumni Event - September

Presentation of the vehicle to various alumni on campus during Homecoming.
Orientation Week Team Display - September

Presentation of the vehicle to frosh students as part of an introduction to engineering student teams on campus.
Student Life 101 - July

A presentation of the vehicle at campus orientation event for 1st year students and their families.
ESQ Summer Camp Tours - June through August

An interactive presentation of vehicle to young summer campers on campus on a weekly basis throughout the summer.
Keystone Campaign Event - June

Presentation of the vehicle during a campaign program celebration for staff and supporters on campus.
You @ Waterloo Day (Open House) - May

Presentation of the vehicle to visiting high school students during the campus open house.
Kids Explorations Day - May

Presentation of the vehicle to visiting young students and their families on campus.
March Break Open House & Explorations - March

We set up an exhibit of the car for high school students interested in attending the university next year, and for visiting young students and their families on campus.
